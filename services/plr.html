What is Past Life Regression?

Past Life Regression (PLR) is a therapeutic technique that uses guided hypnosis to access memories from previous lifetimes. This powerful healing modality helps you understand the root causes of current life challenges, phobias, relationship patterns, and unexplained emotions by exploring their origins in past lives.

Through gentle guidance and deep relaxation, you'll be able to access these memories safely and explore how past life experiences may be influencing your present circumstances. This understanding can lead to profound healing, resolution of long-standing issues, and greater peace in your current life.

Benefits of Past Life Regression

Emotional Healing

Pattern Recognition

Identify and understand recurring patterns in relationships and life circumstances

Phobia Resolution

Discover and resolve unexplained fears and phobias from past life experiences

Life Purpose Clarity

Gain insights into your soul's journey and current life purpose

Frequently Asked Questions

Is past life regression real or just imagination?

Whether past life memories are literal past lives or symbolic representations of the unconscious mind, the healing benefits are very real. The therapeutic value comes from the insights gained and the emotional release experienced during the session.

Will I be unconscious during hypnoheal?

No, you remain fully conscious and aware throughout the session. Hypnoheal is a state of deep relaxation where you're more receptive to positive suggestions, but you're always in control and can respond if needed.

How many sessions will I need?

Some people find resolution in a single session, while others benefit from multiple sessions to explore different aspects or work through complex issues. We'll discuss your needs during the initial consultation.

Are these sessions safe?

Yes, when conducted by a trained professional. I use gentle, safe techniques and create a supportive environment. You remain in control throughout the session and can stop at any time.
