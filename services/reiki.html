What is Reiki Healing?

Reiki is a gentle yet powerful form of energy healing that originated in Japan. The word "Reiki" combines "Rei" (universal life force) and "Ki" (energy) to mean "universal life force energy." This healing modality works by channeling universal energy through the practitioner's hands to promote healing, balance, and well-being.

During a Reiki session, you remain fully clothed while the practitioner places their hands lightly on or near your body, allowing the healing energy to flow where it's needed most. This natural healing energy works on physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual levels to restore balance and promote optimal health.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Reiki feel like?

Most people experience deep relaxation and a sense of peace during Reiki. You may feel warmth, tingling, or gentle energy flowing through your body. Some people feel nothing physically but experience emotional release or mental clarity.

How many Reiki sessions do I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your goals and current state. Some people feel significant benefits after just one session, while others benefit from a series of sessions for deeper healing. We'll discuss your needs during the initial consultation.

Can Reiki be done remotely?

Yes! Distance Reiki is just as effective as in-person sessions. Energy is not limited by physical distance, and many clients experience profound healing through remote sessions.

Is Reiki safe for everyone?

Reiki is completely safe for people of all ages and health conditions. It's gentle, non-invasive, and works in harmony with your body's natural healing processes. It can be used alongside conventional medical treatments.
