What is Tarot Reading?

Tarot reading is an ancient divination practice that uses a deck of 78 cards to provide guidance, insight, and spiritual wisdom. Each card carries symbolic meanings that, when interpreted by an experienced reader, can offer profound insights into your life's journey, relationships, career, and personal growth.

Our tarot sessions are conducted in a sacred, supportive environment where you can explore your questions and receive guidance for your path forward. Whether you're seeking clarity on a specific situation or general life guidance, tarot can provide the wisdom you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are tarot readings?

Tarot readings provide guidance and insights based on the energy and circumstances present at the time of the reading. The accuracy depends on your openness and the reader's skill. Think of it as a spiritual GPS - it shows you the path, but you choose how to walk it.

Can tarot predict the future?

Tarot doesn't predict a fixed future, but rather shows potential outcomes based on current energies and choices. It's a tool for guidance that helps you make informed decisions about your path forward.

How long does a typical session last?

Reading sessions typically last 30-90 minutes depending on the type of reading and your needs. Teaching sessions can be 60-120 minutes to allow for practice and learning.

Do I need to bring anything to the session?

Just bring an open mind and your questions. I provide all the tarot decks and materials needed for the session. You may want to bring a notebook to record insights.
